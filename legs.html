Have you ever been intimidated to work out at the gym because you don't really know any exercises? Well we are here to make it easier for you. You can easily click on the body part you want to work on and then get a how to video.

The Squat

How to:

  1. Step 1: Stand straight with feet hip-width apart.
  2. Step 2: Tighten your stomach muscles
  3. Step 3: Lower down, as if sitting in an invisible chair
  4. Step 4: Straighten your legs to lift back up

The Lunge

How to:

  1. Step 1: Start by standing up tall.
  2. Step 2: Step forward with one foot until your leg reaches a 90-degree angle.
  3. Step 3: Lift your front lunging leg to return to the starting position.
  4. Step 4: Repeat 10 to 12 reps on one leg, or switch off between legs until you've totaled 10 to 12 reps per leg.

Calf Raises
